Data Mapping

Data Mapping is the process of organizing the flow of data within your organization. We will map the entire procedure wherein personal data is collected, processed, stored, and eventually deleted. With Data Mapping, we will be able to determine the specific departments and employees wherein the data comes in and comes out.

View more

Data Purpose, Retention, Deletion

The UK GDPR heavily emphasizes that organizations that processes personal data must establish the lawful purpose of collecting data, the duration of its retention and the process involved in deleting the data once its purpose has been served. We can help you in writing and integrating UK GDPR compliant policies to ensure the legality of your organization’s personal data collection.

View more

Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)

Standard Contractual Clauses are agreements that must be made whenever personal data is shared with other entities or organizations that is not based in the UK. Standard Contractual Clauses are the legal protection that your organization will rely on to ensure that all personal data that is being shared are properly managed and processed.

View more

Data Impact Assessment

We can conduct regular data impact assessments to evaluate the efficiency of your organizations personal data collection and processing. With regular Data Impact Assessments, we will be able to determine where and when potential problems will arise.

View more

Gap Analysis

Gap analysis is a mandatory procedure that all data collectors and processors must conduct. Gap analysis allows organizations to detect and determine the gaps where potential UK GDPR issues might arise and address them properly. We are equipped with the necessary tools and expertise to conduct effective and efficient Gap analysis for your organization.

View more

Third Party Procurements

Charitable organizations heavily rely on third parties in processing personal data especially in collecting donations. And as per UK GDPR, organizations must ensure that third parties involved in the processing of data must adhere to the standards set by the law. We can help you in ensuring that your organization will only partner with UK GDPR compliant third parties through our audit and inspection procedures.
