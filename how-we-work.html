How we work

Three-Steps to GDPR Compliance

At Alexander Crest Ltd, we provide a full range of data protection, GDPR and privacy law services vdesigned to support your b usiness to achieve and maintain compliance through friendly, concise, and effective consultancy, implementation and management.

Talioring our service to fit your business

We offer every client a tailored service designed to meet their business's needs, following a 3-step process designed to ensure each client receives the highest level of service, compliance with the latest regulations, and that their customer's privacy is protected.

Step 1

Initial Assessment

  1. Understand your current position.

  2. Identify the support required.

  3. Develop a scope of work to ensure compliance.

Our team of data privacy legal experts will work with your organisation to understand your current position regarding GDPR and compliance through analysis of existing agreements, policies and procedures and speaking to key stakeholders, including management and key employees.

From that understanding we develop, our friendly team will identify the support your organisation requires to meet GDPR and data privacy law and where Alexander Crest Ltd can offer support from its expert team of legal privacy experts. This assessment will lead to an agreed scope of work that our consultants can undertake on your behalf.
