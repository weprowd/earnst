The benefits

Understanding your responsibility

Our complimentary data compliance assessment will help you to understand your responsibility as a data controller and what your business needs to do to ensure compliance with GDPR and for the protection of your customer's privacy rights.

Ensuring best practise for you and your customers

Our legal data privacy experts will ensure your business, its policies, operational procedures, and business practise meet GDPR. Ensuring you meet all data privacy and GDPR legislation whilst serving and protecting the rights of your customers.

Identification of areas you need to improve

Failing to meet GDPR compliance can be costly. The threat of reputational damage and financial penalties are real. Our team of expert consultants will identify areas that your organisation needs to improve to ensure full compliance with the regulations reducing the risk to your business of a potential breach.

How it works

Our team of data privacy legal experts will work with your organisation to understand your current position regarding GDPR and compliance through analysis of existing agreements, policies and procedures and speaking to key stakeholders, including management and key employees.

Assessing Risk

We will identify potential risks to your organisation and how they can be managed. Minimising risk through operational best practice, binding policies and effective data privacy management can protect your organisation from potentially damaging data breaches.

Training Needs

Our friendly team of consultants can identify skill gaps within your organisation that are essential to implementing policies and procedures that meet GDPR legislation. Our complimentary data assessment will recommend a suite of training solutions designed to upskill your organisation, meet best practice, and ensure full legal compliance.

Winning New Business 

Ensuring compliancy is essential to securing new business, whether entering a tendering process or handling a new client, many will now require examples of how your organisation protects data and how your business complies with GDPR legislation.

Avoid Penalties

Our expert team will work to identify areas of improvement to avoid the risk of fines, reputational damage and loss of customers. 

Get in touch with our friendly team to discuss how we can assist you today or request a complementary data assessment for a free GDPR health check.
