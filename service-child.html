Data Protection Impact Assessment

If you want a comprehensive Data Protection Impact Assessment, we at Alexander Crest will be able to provide you with the most comprehensive and detailed Data Protection Impact Assessment for a single or all of your data processes.

We have a special team composed of experts; legal experts for the interpretation of the UK GDPR and technical experts that can assess and evaluate the strength and weaknesses of your current data management and processing systems.

Get an Impact Assessment

Supporting your business to remain compliant

As a business operating in the UK, Article 25 of the UK GDPR requires you to undergo a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) for accountability purposes. However, aside from accountability purposes, opting for a Data Protection Impact Assessment for all of your data processes will allow you to identify the risks and the extent of your company’s exposure to such risks.

Consultation

Data Protection Impact Assessment is a process that every business that deals with personal data must undergo to. It is a complicated process for most business owners and can sometimes be confusing.

Alexander Crest offers free consultation that will help you understand the purpose and importance of undergoing Data Protection Impact Assessment. We will be more than happy to educate you with the benefits that your company will enjoy with DPIAs.

Flexibility

Data Protection Impact Assessments covers every aspect of your business. Here at Alexander Crest, we can work in your pace and take it step by step. We offer single or multiple DPIAs depending on your preference.

We understand the limitations and possible delay that DPIAs can cause. We are one with you in ensuring that your company will maintain its optimal operations while undergoing DPIA.

Extensive DPIA

Whether it is physical or virtual data, our DPIA will make sure to assess, audit and evaluate each process involved in your company’s data processing. We ensure to deliver our services at the highest quality possible. Our legal experts and technical experts work hand in hand to come up with the most accurate and detailed DPIA for your company.

Risk Management

Alexander Crest always has your company’s best interests. Our Data Protection Impact assessment will not just be for accountability purposes, but for policy and system improvement as well. Our detailed report will contain suggestions and advisory statements that will not just identify the risks involved but also help you improve your current data management and processing systems.
