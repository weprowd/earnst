GDPR Data Breach Service

Expert support from Alexander Crest when you need it most.

Our rapid response team of legal data experts are here to support your business when a data breach occurs. Providing a fast, effective, and compliant approach to meet the 72-hour reporting deadline. Allowing you to focus on your business operations with minimal disruption.

Contact us

Our Service

Assess 

Our team of experts will work with your business to identify the nature and scope of the breach, how it happened and the implications.

Manage

Management of the entire process, ensuring your company conforms to the requirements throughout the reporting process.

Identify 

Our team will identify whether if by law, the data breach requires reporting to the Information Commissioners Office under GDPR legislation.

Communicate

Handle all communication between your business, the relevant authorities and individuals who may have been affected.

Report

We will prepare a report and its submission to the relevant authorities, providing your business with expert representation throughout the entire process.

Remedy

Develop clear and practical plans for remedial action to avoid a recurrence of a future data breach.
