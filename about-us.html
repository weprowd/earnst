Tailored support for every business

We understand that a one size fits all approach does not work. That is why our clients choose Alexander Crest Ltd. A small, dedicated and caring team of data privacy legal experts that offer a tailored package of services designed around the specific industry, size of company and needs of the client and their customers.

At Alexander Crest Ltd, we provide a full range of data protection, GDPR and privacy law services, including the development, management, and review of GDPR compliant policies, agreements, procedures, and protocols through to third party due diligence, support managing client access requests and support handling a data breach

Serving a wide range of clients in various ways, from complete GDPR compliance as the appointed data protection officer to developing individual agreements and policies. Our service is designed to offer you as much support as required to allow your business to thrive.
